Welcome to S2S

S2S are a waste electronics and electrical goods recycling company, based in Rotherham in Yorkshire in the UK.

Using radio frequency identification tags, the company offer a fully traceable and secure service for data erasure,
repairing and recycling of corporate clients end of life electronics.

You are a new employee, you will be responsible for analysis of the data collected from these tracked processes.
You need to understand the business, and begin with how phones are processed.
