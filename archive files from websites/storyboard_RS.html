S2S Process Storyboard

  • 1: Get items
  • 2: Items arrive
  • 3: Start processing
  • 4: Locked/unknown loop
  • 5: Functional test
  • 6: Data erasure
  • 7: Value estimate
  • 8: Blind bidding
  • 9: Sold
  • 10:Sale complete

Sales contact customer to arrange collection of waste electronics,
sales inform shop floor and arrange collection

Shop floor receive items or collect them
Sales check goods are as expected
Shop floor begin processing phones


Associate phone with RFID tag, log status and processes on asset tracking system.
Grade according to visual inspection.
Locked or unknown phones require additional loop.


Shop floor inform sales iPhone locked and quarantine phone.
Sales inform customer to arrange unlock.
Sales confirm unlock to shop floor to continue with processing.


Connect tag to phone, carry out functional test.
Process 10 phones to this stage before continuing to data erasure.


Functional test is complete by carrying out data erasure.
When the asset tracking process is complete,
phones are labelled and placed in secure stores.
Completed asset tracking report is exported to excel and sent to sales.


Sales recive excel report including status and estimated value of phone.
Excel sheet sent to customer.
Customer confirms which items they want back, or to sell.


Inform finance and shop floor which items are purchased or returned.
Export new excel sheet with items for sale to external buyers.
Email list to buyers for 72hrs blind bidding.


Successful bidder confirmed. System updated and sales inform shop floor.
Shop floor locate items and certificates for sending to buyer.
Items packaged and sent to buyer.


Shop floor confirm items sent.
Sales update reports and buyer information.
